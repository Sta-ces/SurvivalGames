Hail to Gods (5) = 1% of chance to multiply your score.
Second Chance (25) = 1 extra life (1 time each game).
Automatic Shoot (50) = Shoot Automaticly without used the trigger.
Cool Down (100) = Slow down time during 5 seconds (Can use the all 20 kills).
Triple Shoot (125) = Shoots in 3 different directions.
Shield (150) = Protect you for 1 hit.
Shockwave (200) = Kill all the enemies in a certain range (Can use the all 30 kills).
End of the game (500) = Kill the last 50 enemies and finish the game.
